Relevant Projects

Smart Home

Home Assistant is my to go platform for smart home integration. I run Home Assistant on Docker in Raspberry Pi 4. I chose Home Assistant because i am able to integrate smart devices from different brands into one platform. This way, i don't have to open multiple apps if i want to control or get data from sensors of different brand.

Recently i have also added Zigbee coordinator to Home Assistant. This enables me to move zigbee devices previously tied to specific brand platform into Home Assistant. I get almost no device downtime now compared to the previous configuration.

Another advantage of using Home Assistant is that i can expose the devices on Home Assistant to Apple Home platform, a big win for iOS users. I can now also use Siri to control my home devices.
